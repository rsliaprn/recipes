Watermelon Icecream

You should try to make it yourself! It's tastes like watermelon, but actually frosty

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Watermelon juice
  • Green stuff

Steps

  1. Froze some milk
  2. Add a little bit of watermelon in the process
  3. Put it in the green stuff
  4. All is done!
